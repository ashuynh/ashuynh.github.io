About Me

My name is Ashley Huynh and I am an AME student attending at Phillip Burton Highschool of San Francisco, CA. I've so far had 3 years of standard art classes and one year of Digital Media Arts (DMA) throughout my time in middle and high school. I have always grown up to liking and learning arts with a dream of becoming a digital artist. But recently I’ve gone off to being unsure if I really do want to have that kind of career in the future. Art is more of a thing I’d enjoy doing but I haven’t thought of it as something I truly aspire to be. I’m hoping that as I continue my studies with art that it’ll help me in confirming my choice about the upcoming concerns of how much I want art to be in my life.

Sunken Blooms

This was a project that I personally made for myself just for the fun of it.
It turned out so great that I decided to add this to my website. I first
made a couple cherry blossoms and decided to increasde their scale
and later pictured a girl wearing them as hair pieces. The most
challenging part of this was trying to fit the colors together; I wanted
pink for the cherry blossoms and purple looked pretty nice with it in her
hair, but I started to realize I repeatedly used the same few colors and
needed to make it pop with new ones. A small thing I added was the
falling pedals within the girl (might not be too obvious though) and I
decided to do that as I was trying to mix some colors into her. I decided
to keep it this way because it made me thought of all her feelings floating
down into her heart, piling into so many emotions that she feels like she's
drowning in them. That feeling gave me the idea of setting her under water.
To me, she looks beautiful outside but inside she is sinking under all of her emotions.

Character Design

This project was assignment called Character Design where we designed and drew our own characters. This was actually based on an outline I drew on my phone about aq year ago and decided to use it for this assignment. I downloaded it on my computer and traced and colored in this work. The colors are a bit off because I just plastered whatever colors seemed nice at the moment. My original thought was of brown hair with light and dark blue clothing. This project was very time consuming because of all of the lines that I had to trace in.Something that I'd want to give more improvement on this project would be color choice.

Social Justice Poster

This assignment involved the consideration of big matters which aren't
being as well adressed as it should be. I decided to cover this topic with
human contribution of CO2 in our ecosystems through man's creations
of air-disturbing machines, cigarettes and fires. The world's health means
a lot to me because without carng for it, it'll affect all populations. The
world is our beauty and home, represented in the rose. We decide whether
to care for it or burn it. People don't realize hwo big of in impact everyone
does to this issue. Even just a small act of conservatation can go a long
way if everyone did the same.

Event Poster

This image was from an assignment of making an event poster. I immediately thought of making a firework event because they are spectacular to view during the night. Soon after, it was mentioned that everyone should finish this assignment based on a video that teaches you along the way. If you made your own, it counted as extra credit,so I decided to make this work based on my own ideas. I first began searching up annual firework events and later found one from Japan. They had cherry blossoms and Japanese towers, which I've put into my art. I wanted it to have a warm and nice feeling so I added some magenta and, blue, and purple. To pop out of this work, I made sparks for the fireworks with ombres of white, pink, and red.
